About Us

The IIPS Alumni Association exists to help you stay connected to IIPS, and to each other,wherever you are in your life now. Whatever you're seeking, an excuse to come back for reunion,a job lead or a mind-expanding magazine, we've got you covered.


Services we provide:

  • Alumni directory
  • Alumni Facebook page
  • Comming Reunions
  • Connect Your Carrier
  • Alumni Centre

Our Mission:

  • To promote and foster belongingness and harmony between the Alumni and the Alma Mater.
  • To encourage formation of chapters to increase participation of the Alumni.
  • To encourage the Alumni to take abiding interest in the progress and development of the Alma Mater.
  • To undertake activities of nation building including those of charitable nature.
  • To undertake all such lawful activities as are conducive to the attainment of the above objectives.
  • To generate corpus at the Devi Ahilya University for creating better educational/recreational facilities.
  • To exchange professional knowledge, organize technical conferences, seminars workshops, training courses, etc.
  • To accept/receive donations for fulfillment of the objectives.
