Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common alumni queries.

If your question isn't answered here, please contact the Alumni Office.

My details

How do I update my contact details?
Simply fill in our secure online update form with your new details. Your information will be updated in the IIPS alumni database . Please note that you will need to have an account for IIPS Alumni Online to access the form. You can register here, you will be able to update your details online.

Oxford Alumni Online

How do I register for Oxford Alumni Online?
Alumni can sign up for a free account by filling in the registration form.

The University knows I am an alumna/alumnus but I can't log in.

Have you registered for the site? Please note that for your security we do not automatically register alumni for Alumni Online. Even if you regularly receive communications from us you will need to complete the registration form to access the service. If you are unsure please contact us
